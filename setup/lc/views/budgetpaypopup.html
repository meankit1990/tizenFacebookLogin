Budget Pay

What is BudgetPay?

BudgetPay is a payment option that allows you to spread the total cost of an item into three installments at no additional charge, making shopping for Exceptional Quality, Exquisite Designs, and Outstanding value even more convenient!
Note: Store credit wont be enabled

Why is the first BudgetPay payment installment higher than the other 2 payments?

The price of the item will be divided into three equal payments. The first payment will include all Shipping & Handling and taxes. Your first payment will always be higher than the remaining two payments because of the additional charges for Shipping & Handling and taxes.

When are payment installments due?

The first payment will be charged to your selected method of payment on the day your order is placed. The second payment will be charged to the same selected method of payment 30 days after the day your order is placed, and the third and final payment will be processed 60 days after the day your order is placed.

How can I pay the balance of remaining installments?

Once your order has shipped, you can pay the total remaining installment balance anytime by contacting our Customer Service Department at {{customerCareNumber}} . Please have your invoice and order numbers with you to expedite your request.
