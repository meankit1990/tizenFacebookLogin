How to buy

  • Step 1 : Enter valid phone number, select Choice/Qty & click order now.
  • Step 2 : Now you need to login to order product.
  • Step 3 : If you are not a signed up user call {{customerCareNumber}} to setup your account.
  • Step 4 : For existing user : Confirm shipping/billing address, shipping method and click continue on address screen.
  • Step 5 : To edit quantity you can click on Item in bag on address screen and update.
  • Step 6 : Check total amount and price breakup and click continue on order screen.
  • Step 7 : Confirm card details & click pay now button on payment screen.
  • Step 8 : Order confirmation/failure screen is shown. The confirmation will be sent to your registered email id as well.
